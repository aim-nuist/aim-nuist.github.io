Institute for Artificial Intelligence in Medicine(AIM)

We are AIM, the institute for artificial intelligence in Medicine.We are the joint research institute of Nanjing university of information science and technology and Zhongda Hospital. We are dedicated to push the frontiers of precision medicine and healthcare by leveraging the artificial intelligence and big medical data.
